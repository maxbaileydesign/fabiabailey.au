Behind the Screen

Book One · Chapter One
© Fabia Bailey, 2026

I'm going to tell you about a guy I never quite had, and a life I somehow ended up with anyway.

There's a version of this story I could tell you where I give you everything upfront. Where I say: here is how it begins, and here is how it ends, and here is the person I become in between. I could make it sound like a straight line because from here it almost looks like one.

But that's not the version I'm telling. It was more like — you know when you're trying to find a song on the radio and you keep getting static, and then briefly you catch it, and then you lose it again, and then suddenly it's there and it's clear and it's been playing all along? It was like that. A lot of static. Brief, disorienting moments of clarity. And then, eventually, the signal.

I knew his typing rhythm before I knew the sound of his voice.

About the Author

Fabia
Bailey

Fabia Bailey is a British-Australian writer born in Shoreham-by-Sea, England, raised in Surrey, and now based in Sydney, Australia.

She spent twelve years working in marketing for global companies including Microsoft, Rugby Australia and Colgate-Palmolive — a career that took her across industries and countries, and gave her a professional life she was good at and eventually chose to step back from.

Since becoming a mother of two boys, she has focused on family and the creative projects she had always set aside for later. Online Friends is her debut novel series — a book she has been carrying for most of her adult life, and one she finally had the time and the distance to write properly.
